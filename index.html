Join the Electric Revolution: Driving Towards a Greener Future!

Vision

The club envisions to be a leading force in e-mobility in Nigeria, ensuring a future where electric vehicles are seamlessly integrated into the society, reducing carbon emissions and enhancing air quality thus shaping a more sustainable future for generations to come

Purpose

The purpose of the Electric Vehicle Club is to catalyze positive change by promoting electric vehicles, sustainable practices, and renewable energy solutions. We aim to provide a dynamic platform for students to explore EV technology, engage in impactful projects, and foster a culture of environmental consciousness. By collaborating with diverse stakeholders and advocating for policy changes, we seek to influence the broader community towards a more sustainable and electrified future.

Mission

The Electric Vehicle Club is committed to advancing sustainable transportation solutions and promoting the adoption of electric vehicles (EVs) in Nigeria. Through innovative projects, research and outreach, we aim to inspire environmental stewardship, drive technological innovation and create a greener and more sustainable future for our community and beyond
